Culinary Chronicles: Showcasing the Best Food in the World

A website by: Kate Prothero

Indian Cuisine

This is my favourite type of cuisine, I love to eat in Indian restaurants, I love to cook Indian food. The flavours are warm and welcoming, with many more tradional curries much healtheir than you'd pick up in your local takeaway. Onion, garlic, garam masala, cumin, chilli, the list goes on. My 3 favourite dishes are as follows;

  • Chicken dhansak
  • Tamarind fries
  • Chat bombs

Mexican Cuisine

Second on the list is Mexican food, but only when fresh. For me, mexican food does not keep well. My family and I love nothing more than a super spicy Mexican feast on a Friday night, chilli, lime, avocados and beans. Yum yum!

  • Mexican chicken stew
  • Enchildas
  • Burrito bowl

Greek Cuisine

My third favourite cuisine has to be Greek. They say a Mediterranean diet is the best around, and for me, Greek has it all. Grilled meats, feta cheese, olives and flat breads, it just reminds me of being on holiday.

  • A traditional greek salad
  • Chicken souvlaki
  • Keftedes
